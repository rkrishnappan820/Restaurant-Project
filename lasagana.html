Lasagna

Here at Rahul's we as a team pride ourselves upon perfection, and our Lasagna is that.
The Lasagna we make here is made with meat sauce as the base and layer sauce with the
noodles and cheese then until baking. Lasagna is great to serve a large family or group of people and freezes well too
and with its low cost of $7 (depending upon size) it's a great choice for anyone.
